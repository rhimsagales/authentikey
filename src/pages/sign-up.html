Privacy Policy for Authentikey

Effective Date: January 22, 2025


Introduction

Authentikey is committed to protecting the privacy of its users. This privacy policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your personal information in the context of our project, which aims to provide a secure and efficient solution for managing student access to computers in academic environments.


1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of personal information:

  • Student ID
  • Password
  • Name
  • Section
  • Course
  • Year Level
  • Email
  • Record of logs

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Identification: Personal information such as Student ID, name, section, and email is used for user identification.
  • Logs Display: Records of logs are displayed on the webpage to monitor access and usage.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information with unauthorized third parties. However, your personal data is stored on MongoDB Atlas for database management. Additionally, personal data may be displayed during demonstrations to authorized school personnel, such as professors, for educational purposes.


4. Data Protection

We take the protection of your data seriously and use encryption to safeguard your password.


5. Your Rights

Users have the right to:

  • Directly update personal information, excluding student computer usage logs.
  • Delete the account immediately without prior consent.
  • Request corrections for inaccuracies in the student's computer usage logs.

6. Contact Information

For any queries regarding this policy, please contact us at authentikey.icct.contact@gmail.com.


Conclusion

Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to handling your data responsibly. If you have any concerns about how your data is managed, please reach out to the project administrators.
